We Encourage & Empower Individuals

Inclusion. Self-Determination. Independence

Every individual deserves all three. Innovative Services, Inc. is committed to making a positive impact on the lives of individuals with intellectual and developmental disability as well as various other medical, mental, and behavioral health diagnoses. We find the right residence that meets each individual’s needs in the community of their choice and then provide the support and security to help them maximize their potential.
